Generate a batch of vouchers

Some things to remember:

  • Vouchers can be deleted if any mistake has been made here.
  • Vouchers can't be deleted after publishing to a Sales Channel*1, but that's a next step after this.
  • There is no automatic amount validations so make sure there is money on the wallet to cover the vouchers otherwise we will in big trouble.
  • Once generated correctly, vouchers have to be published to become available on a Sale Channel*1

  • *1 Sales Channel is a way to sell products, e.g. Shopify, Facebook, Instagram, PoS.
  • *2Called "count"
  • *3Total amount to spend. Each voucher will contain amount = (total amount)/count, also total amount has to be divisible by count)
